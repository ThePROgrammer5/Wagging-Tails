ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Jakob, and I'm a responsible and animal-loving 13-year-old who's excited to start my own dog walking, sitting, and overnight stay business this summer. As a passionate animal lover, I've had the pleasure of caring for many furry friends in my neighborhood. With over 7 years of experience dog sitting for my friends' and family members' pets, I've developed a strong understanding of canine behavior, habits, and needs. I'm excited to bring this experience to your pets!

Why choose me?

As a young entrepreneur, I'm eager to provide top-notch care for your pets. I'm reliable, trustworthy, and enthusiastic about animals. Plus, my youthful energy means I can keep up with even the most playful pups!As a teenager, I'm available to work flexible hours during the summer, including evenings and weekends. I'll provide regular updates and photos so you can stay connected with your pet while they're in my care.
